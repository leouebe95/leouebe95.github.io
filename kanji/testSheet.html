Type short sentences to practice kanji reading and writing. This page is meant to be printed for practice. When printing, the input text and explanation will be hidden. Phonetic kana should follow the kanji in parenthesis, like in this example: 足（あし）のつめ. Each sentence will be displayed vertically, right to left. A blank line starts a new row of sentences.

There are 4 display modes:

  • Kanji: Find the right kanji to fill the blanks.
  • Trace: Kanji is displayed in grey to practice writing
  • Kana: Find the phonetic kana for all the kanji
  • Solution: Display both the kanji and phonetic kana
